Code of conduct

tl;dr be respectful of others

If you harass, abuse or create an unsafe or inappropriate environment for participants, we'll ask you to leave.
If you don't comply with these guidelines, we'll involve security and / or local law enforcement.

We're hackers: we understand the nature of the stuff we work on (electronics, heat, etc). But we expect participants to maintain a safe, professional environment at all times. If it's illegal outside the hackathon, it's definitely illegal inside the hackathon.

Attendees should not use sexualized images, activities, or other material both in their hacks and during the event.

If what you’re doing is making someone feel uncomfortable, that counts as harassment and is enough reason to stop doing it.
